Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about careers at Silverfest Cleaning Services.

What qualifications do I need to work at Silverfest?

Qualifications vary by position. For cleaning roles, we value reliability, attention to detail, and a strong work ethic. For management positions, we typically look for relevant experience and leadership skills. All specific requirements are listed in our job descriptions.

What is the interview process like?

Our interview process typically includes an initial phone screening, followed by an in-person or virtual interview. For some positions, we may include a practical assessment or second interview. We focus on understanding your experience, skills, and how you would fit with our team culture.

Do you provide training?

Yes, all new employees receive comprehensive training specific to their role. We believe in continuous development, so ongoing training opportunities are available to help you grow your skills and advance your career with us.

What are the working hours?

Working hours vary by position and client needs. We offer full-time, part-time, and flexible scheduling options. Many cleaning positions include early morning, evening, or weekend hours. Administrative and management roles typically follow standard business hours.

How long does the application process take?

Our goal is to complete the hiring process within 2-3 weeks from application to offer. However, this timeline can vary based on the position and volume of applications. We strive to keep all candidates informed throughout the process.
